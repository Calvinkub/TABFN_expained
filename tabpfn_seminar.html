The idea

You never train on your data — it's one forward pass

  • Tabular data is everywhere, yet gradient-boosted trees (XGBoost, CatBoost) still win.
  • TabPFN = a Transformer pretrained once on millions of synthetic datasets, then frozen.
  • Feed your whole table + test rows as one prompt → predictions in ~1 second, no tuning.
  • It replaces the entire AutoML pipeline with a single forward pass.
A whole AutoML stack (tuning, ensembling) → replaced by one TabPFN forward pass. (via Max Kuhn, R/Pharma 2025)
